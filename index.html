About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in CRIPAC, NLPR, Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by Prof. Zhaoxiang Zhang. I am also an intern at TuSimple supervised by Dr. Naiyan Wang and Dr. Feng Wang from May 2020 to April 2023. I got my bachelor's degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU) in 2019, majoring in automation. My research interests focus on the perception algorithm in autonomous driving scenarios.
